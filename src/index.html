This calculator is meant to serve as long term estimate for your mining return. The network difficulty and Chia/USD exchange rate changes over time.
Proof of storage mining is a good way to get a passive income and to get some use out of that unused hard drive space.
Linus has some good thoughts on building a cheap server with loots of space to mine with.
"We're building a blockchain based on proofs of space and time to make a cryptocurrency which is less wasteful, more decentralized, and more secure."
The calculator calculates expected Chia mined with whatever hard drive space you specify. It does not take into account the expected increast of difficulty over time.
Proof-of-space (PoS), also known as Proof-of-capacity (PoC), is a way of showing that you have a legitimate interest in a service by dedicating a chosen amount of memory or hard drive space to solve a problem given by the service provider.
Proof of space is similar to proof of work, used by the Bitcoin or Ethereum networks. Instead of CPU or GPU power, hard drive storage is used. Proof-of-space is loosely related to memory-hard functions and proofs of retrievability.
